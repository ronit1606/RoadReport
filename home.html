Welcome to the Traffic Police website!

Our mission is to ensure the safety and efficiency of our roads, and to uphold the laws and regulations that govern them. We are committed to promoting responsible driving habits and reducing the number of accidents on our roads.

Here are some of the key features of our website:

  • Traffic Alerts: Stay up-to-date on the latest traffic conditions and road closures in your area.
  • Driver Resources: Get information on traffic laws, driver safety tips, and licensing requirements.
  • Vehicle Registration: Register your vehicle online, and find out about vehicle inspection requirements.
  • Online Payments: Pay traffic fines and other fees online, quickly and easily.
  • Contact Us: Have a question or concern? Contact us through our online form or by phone.
